#### Data Collection

Rep5x is an academic research project conducted at the University of Twente as part of a Master's thesis in Industrial Design Engineering. We collect minimal data necessary for research purposes and community support:

- Community interactions on Discord and GitHub for research analysis
- Build reports and performance data submitted voluntarily by users
- Website analytics to understand project reach and engagement
- Contact form submissions for project support
- Email addresses for project updates (optional subscription only)

All data collection follows University of Twente ethics guidelines and EU GDPR requirements.

#### Use of Information

Collected data is used solely for:

- Academic research into 5-axis printing adoption and barriers
- Improving project documentation and resources
- Supporting community members with technical questions
- Analysing project impact and user adoption barriers for Master's thesis research

No personal data is shared with third parties or used for commercial purposes.

#### Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- Access is limited to authorised research personnel
- Data is anonymised for research analysis where possible
- Standard security practices are followed for data storage and transmission

#### Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data we hold
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing
- Receive a copy of your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rep5x.com or through the contact form on this website.

#### Research Data

This project may collect anonymous usage data and build reports for research purposes. Participation is voluntary and you can opt out at any time. Research findings may be published in academic papers with all personal information removed.

#### Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as the project develops. Significant changes will be announced through project communication channels. Continued participation implies acceptance of updated terms.

#### Contact

For privacy concerns or questions, you can contact us through:

- Discord community: [Join Discord](https://discord.gg/75Hy5dMwTJ)
- Website contact form: [Contact Us](/contact)
- University of Twente research ethics board for formal complaints
